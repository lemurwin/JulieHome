About Me

Julie Moon is a musician, studying and exploring music through the roles of performer, improviser, and educator.

She is currently working on a theater project as well as an album with her electro-acoustic duo, OMMO.  As an educator, she incorporates creative experimentation and improvisation into the learning experience.

As a performer, she strives to deliver music with the gestural authenticity and physicality of improvisation whereas aspects of her improvisational vocabulary are derived from studies in piano music. Her current compositions are aligned with personal explorations that invite listeners to experience these emotional journeys themselves.
